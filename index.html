COVID-19 Epidemic in Malaysia

Cases, Deaths & Vaccinations by State

Explore the progression of the COVID-19 pandemic across Malaysia's states from 2020 onwards. This visualization combines official data from CPRC, the CPRC Hospital System, MKAK, and MySejahtera to reveal patterns of disease spread, mortality, and vaccination efforts.

Each state is represented as a radial "feather" visualization, where the concentric circles represent different time periods. The length and color of segments within each circle encode case counts, death rates, and vaccination coverage.

This multi-layered approach allows us to simultaneously examine temporal trends and geographic disparities across Malaysia's diverse states, from heavily urbanized areas to less populated regions.

Interactive tooltips reveal detailed metrics for each state, time period, and demographic group when you hover over the visualization.

How to Read a COVID Feather

Each circle in the visualization represents a Malaysian state. Within each circle, we find concentric rings representing different time periods in the pandemic timeline.

The radial rings progress outward from the earliest data (center) to the most recent observations. Each ring is divided into multiple segments, allowing us to show several key metrics simultaneously.

The visualization uses color encoding to distinguish between case counts (blue), deaths (red), and vaccination rates (green). The width and length of segments represent the magnitude of each metric relative to other states and time periods.

States with higher case counts or mortality rates show as wider arcs, making it easy to identify which regions were most heavily impacted during different phases of the pandemic.

Hover over any segment to see detailed information including the specific state, time period, exact case count, death toll, vaccination percentage, and other relevant statistics.

Data Sources: Official COVID-19 Malaysia Repository | Powered by CPRC, CPRC Hospital System, MKAK, and MySejahtera
